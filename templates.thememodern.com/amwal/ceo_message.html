Sarthak Mittal- Chairmen Mittal Alliance

As Chairman of Mittal Alliance and CEO of BAD Talks India and Wordplay Ventures U.S.A, I’m dedicated to driving innovation, entrepreneurship, and business growth globally. At Mittal Alliance, I lead initiatives that empower Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), helping them overcome challenges and succeed through strong industrial networks and partnerships. As Co-Founder of Diginomo Pvt. Ltd, I support startups by securing resources like jobs, funding, and skills development.

With extensive experience in corporate leadership and campus recruitment, I bridge the gap between academia and industry, mentoring students, startups, and young professionals. Through BAD Talks, I inspire as a public speaker, while at Wordplay Ventures, I contribute to the creative economy.

I’m also an active thought leader, sharing insights on entrepreneurship, business strategy, and personal growth through my YouTube channel, blogs, and publications. I regularly participate in global forums, advocating for sustainability and innovation as key drivers of long-term success for MSMEs, startups, and students.
