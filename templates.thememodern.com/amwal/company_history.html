COMPANY HISTORY

  1. HOME
  2. COMPANY HISTORY

1907Mittal Group Founded

Mittal Group Founded

1951India's First Rolling Brass Mill Estd.

Mittal's Successfully Founded Indian First Brass Rolling Mill, Followed by Suresh Chandra ji Mittal (Sarthak's Grandfather) and His 3 Brothers (Dinesh ji Mittal, Gopaldas Ji Mittal, Ramesh Ji Mittal), This was the greatest known revolution in Indian Industries

2003Sarthak Mittal was Born

Sarthak Mittal was Born on 1 Dec 2003, This was the 6th Generation of Mittal Family.

2020Mittal Alliance was Founded

Mittal Alliance is a Group of Industries and Businesses working Together in Order to Promote Growth of MSME's & Existing Industries, An Extraordinary Initiative Leaded by Sarthak Mittal, Enriching Industries with Power of AI, Automation & Technology & Promoting Coworking, Sarthak Mittal is the Chairman of This Alliance

2022Anjucreations India

AnjuCreations India was the first Company working with Mittal Alliance, Founded by CEO Anju Mittal (Sarthak's Mother), Anjucreations aims at helping small craftmans and raw products to be sold all over india through ecommerce platforms like Meesho and Flipkart, Sarthak worked as CEO Initially but later he just looked over the technical Operations

2023Young Leaders Community Founded

Now Sarthak Mittal Identified that Students are not getting job due to lack of appropriate Industrial exposure, so he launched young leaders which aim at "Creating Young Leaders", and "Helping Students", Young leaders was successful to reach 1,50,000 Students and 500+ Jobs and Internship in Just 1 Year, Sarthak Mittal Leaded this Initiative as a President at Young Leaders

2024Badbusiness.in Founded

Badbusiness.in a basic concept, which aims at providing right resources at right time to business, we outsource customer need to relevant industries, saving time and enhancing quality. Sarthak is Leading this Initiative as CEO
